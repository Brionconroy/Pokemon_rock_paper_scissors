Rules

This is a rock paper scissors game where as the three differnt typs of pokemon Venusaur, Blastoise, Charizard pick the Pokemon you want to choose by clicking on one of the pictures.
The computer(Team Rocket) will then choose a Pokemon. you can see who won the battle in the result section.

Venusaur = Grass type

Blastoise = water type

Charizard = fire type

Grass beats Water
Water beats Fire
Fire beats Grass
